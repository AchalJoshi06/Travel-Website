The Snaeland Story: Experts in the Arctic Circle

Our mission is simple: To share the breathtaking beauty of the Nordic region through mindful, low-impact travel.

From a single backpack to a dedicated team

Snaeland wasn't founded in a boardroom; it began with a single journey to Iceland and a dedication to the culture and landscape of the North. We focus exclusively on the Arctic Circle countries because we believe deep specialization creates the best experiences.

Every itinerary is hand-crafted by our founders, ensuring authenticity and a sustainable approach. We only run small tours (maximum 12 people) to minimize our footprint and maximize your personal connection with the environment.

Why Choose Snaeland?

Nordic-First Guides

All our guides are certified locals in Iceland, Norway, and the Faroes, providing genuine insight beyond any guidebook.

Small Group Policy

We limit all tours to 12 people max. This ensures lower environmental impact and higher quality, personalized attention for every guest.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. Our prices include flights, accommodation, and two meals a day, so you can focus only on the adventure.
